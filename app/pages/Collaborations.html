Utrecht

By sharing the knowledge of www.synenergene.eu iGEM Utrecht gave us push forward in our work and especially in the human practices area. We could there gain knowledge in how to optimize our social impact by small, but smart, changes. In return we sent Utrecht our company brochure to inspire them and to help them start designing their own brochure.

York

iGEM York served as a helpful guide in the beginning of our iGEM-trip. By mentoring us by giving smart tips and information of value to ease our work they helped us create a platform for iGEM in Lund.
